# ADR Template

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Superseded

## Title

Describe the architectural decision being made.

## Context

Explain the situation, constraints, and business or technical forces driving the decision.

## Decision

Describe the decision that was made.

## Consequences

List the expected trade-offs, risks, and follow-on work.

## Review notes

Capture architecture, security, quality, and release sign-off notes if applicable.
