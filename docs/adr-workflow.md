# ADR Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow defines how architectural decisions are recorded and governed across all future products.

## Workflow

1. Identify a major decision that affects structure, integration, data, quality, or security.
2. Create or update an ADR using the standard template.
3. Review the ADR with the relevant architecture, security, and quality stakeholders.
4. Mark the ADR as accepted, proposed, or superseded.
5. Retain the ADR in the repository as a durable decision record.

## Governance rule

A decision becomes operationally valid only when its ADR is reviewed and accepted by the required governance owners.

## Recommended practice

Keep ADRs small, specific, and evidence-backed. Avoid broad design essays in place of clear decision records.
